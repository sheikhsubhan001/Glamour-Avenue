Pakistan’s largest chain of
beauty training institutes

Glamour Institute is one of Pakistan’s largest chains of beauty training institutes with internationally qualified staff. Some of the best makeup artists, hairdressers, and beauty therapists in Pakistan have their roots and foundations in this prestigious institute. Glamour Institute is the only beauty institute in Pakistan that has foreign affiliations.

Glamour Institute is located in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, and Rawalpindi, and is continuing to expand across the country. This prestigious institute has certified thousands of students who now independently own their own businesses both locally and internationally or are part of leading salon brands. The institute offers state-of-the-art facilities, equipped to cater to the needs of aspiring beauticians.

About City & Guilds

City & Guilds is a well-established brand and a global leader in skills development. Over one million people earn a City & Guilds qualification every year, providing them with the skills they need to thrive in the workplace. In the global economy, the ability to compete depends on building a skilled, competent and confident workforce. City & Guilds understands the value of portable skills and globally recognised standards that enable people and organisations to succeed. It partners with Governments, employers and educators in over 50 countries, across five continents, to advise them on developing high-quality skills training that meets the needs of the global workforce, without compromising on local demands.

City & Guilds Assured Programme

Glamour Institute is the only Institute offering the City & Guilds Assured Programme in Pakistan. This means that City & Guilds has recognized the process and systems used to deliver the programme as well as a review of the content from a learning and development perspective. We have reached this milestone after undergoing a rigorous benchmark model. It is a formal recognition for our Group and learners.

Professional Programme in Hair, Beauty And Skin Aesthetics

Our Professional Programme is being offered under the City & Guilds Assured Programme. It has been designed keeping in view the market need and making the human resource employable. It is a 3 months programme that provides holistic training and career guidance in all areas i.e., cut, colour, styling, makeup, body therapy, skin and hair treatments. The aim is to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge required to excel in the beauty and aesthetics industry, ensuring they are job-ready upon graduation. The curriculum covers advanced techniques and includes hands-on practice with industry-standard tools and products.

Advanced Diploma in Cosmetology

This comprehensive program is designed for aspiring cosmetologists seeking in-depth knowledge and advanced techniques. It covers hair care, styling, cutting, coloring, advanced skincare treatments, makeup artistry, and nail technology. The diploma emphasizes practical application and client consultation skills, preparing students for leadership roles in salons and spas.

Certificate in Professional Makeup Artistry

Focusing on the art of makeup, this certificate program teaches fundamental to advanced makeup techniques for various occasions, including bridal, fashion, and special effects. Students will learn about color theory, facial anatomy, product knowledge, and hygiene, building a strong portfolio for a career as a professional makeup artist.

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must have completed their matriculation (or equivalent) and be at least 16 years old. A passion for the beauty industry and a willingness to learn are essential. Specific programs may have additional prerequisites, which will be detailed during the application process.

Application Process

Prospective students can apply online through our website or visit the institute for an in-person application. The application form requires personal details, educational background, and a statement of purpose. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview and a basic aptitude test. Admissions are granted on a rolling basis, so early application is encouraged.

Career Opportunities
